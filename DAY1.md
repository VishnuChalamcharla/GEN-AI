# GEN-AI – Day 1

## What is Intelligence?
- Observe
- Learn from experience
- Identify patterns
- Make decisions
- Improve over time

## Human Intelligence
- Learns continuously
- Based on observation and experience
- Includes emotions and consciousness

## Artificial Intelligence (AI)
- Imitation of human intelligence
- Learns from data
- No emotions or consciousness

## Role of Data
- Data is the fuel for AI

## Types of Data
### Structured Data
- Tables, databases

### Unstructured Data
- Text, audio, images, video
